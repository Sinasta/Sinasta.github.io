PAVILION 2019

In order to analyze the Wooden Pavilion of Ristola in Lahti on a structural level, it was first necessary to understand the mathematical rules underlying its form. To accomplish this, I needed to model the pavilion as a 3D model and, as a consequence, deduce the harmonic rules of its composition. It was an interesting learning experience to conduct a meticulously detailed analysis of a work whose documentation was entirely missing.
