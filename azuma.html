AZUMA 2018

The analysis of the Hotel Bleston by Azuma architects in Nagano required a certain sensitivity to the architectural language created by the formal composition and the use of certain materials and their workmanship. In this work, I decided to shift the focus from a traditional plan/section analysis to a morpho-phenomenological approach as well as putting attention to the psychological impact on the observers.
