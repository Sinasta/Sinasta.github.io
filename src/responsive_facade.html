RESPONSIVE FACADE

Within the framework of the digital fabrication studio I have concentrated on the experimentation and evaluation of kinetic facades. I started with an analysis of the existing mechanics. The next step was to develop several different prototypes in order to respect some of the advantages and disadvantages and to provide suggestions for improvement. After the experimental phase, the simulation was used to evaluate the advantages regarding the energy balance and the visible light irradiation.
