VOID 2023

Void space is a fundamental element in any urban environment. It provides a sense of calm in the face of dense buildings, traffic and crowds, providing a place for relaxation and meditation. The psychological impact of these spaces is considerable, as they can help reduce stress and improve mental health. Nevertheless, these spaces are often perceived as missed opportunities for development, resulting in even more crowded and oppressive urban environments. The introduction of water features in the city could be a potential solution to this problem.
