ATELIER EXTENSION

The extension of the Lazzaretto Veccio in Venice was our ﬁrst major project within the framework of our studies. It was about the conversion of a fallow land into a studio for renovation students. The challenges were to work with the culture of the Lazzaretto directly beside and also to integrate it into the Venezia skylign without falling into a false old. This project continues the shell of the old building and thus enables an integration into the surrounding area. Furthermore, a larger part of the fallow land will be left untouched and made inaccessible, thus counteracting the uprooting of the site by this project.
