KUROS EXPOSITION

The following project was born from the uniﬁcation of two completely independent intentions: To develop a solution to the increasing homelessness rates and to allow the best possible integration into the ﬂoral and faunal environment. Based on the use of abandoned railway containers, this project offers a cheap and simple way of construction. After an intensive analysis of the context, beneﬁcial interactions with the ﬂora and fauna has been developed.
