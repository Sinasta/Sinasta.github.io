KUROS EXPOSITION 2018

In this work, the main focus was put on the emotional perception of the visitor. The task was to advantageously expose a Kouros and to allow a bridge between architecture and art. My approach was to observe the different effects that daylight has on the surface structure of the statue when it strikes the texture, either directly or indirectly. It was important to analyze the different angles and intensities of the sun.
